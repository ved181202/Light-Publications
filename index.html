COMPANY PROFILE


Light Publications is an innovative, knowledge-driven printing and packaging house with 54 years of experience providing quality print services to our customers all over India. We have evolved into a one-stop shop offering complete solutions ranging from design to printing and packaging. Our customer base includes businesses that export to Europe, Australia, and the USA and also have a strong presence in the domestic market.
Our highly trained team is supported by cutting-edge software and equipment and is dedicated to continuous improvement. The ethos of our company is to add value and technical solutions to quality products and to develop a strong relationship with a deep understanding of the client. Our accomplishments are the result of strong teamwork by a professional workforce whose practical knowledge and experience ensure that all potential client concerns are addressed the moment a job is assigned to us.
